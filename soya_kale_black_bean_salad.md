# Soya, kale and black bean salad

## 
Combine in microwaveable bowl:
- 100g of dehydrated soya
- 1 tin/carton black beans (with the water)
- kale
- chopped courgette

## Cook
Cook in the microwave for 4/5 minutes

## Serve
Mix into bowl:
- soya yoghurt
- harissa paste
- salt
- pepper
- chopped cherry tomatoes
- rocket
